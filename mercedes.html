In profile


modern F1 revival started with the creation of a works squad for 2010 - the platform for a meteoric rise up the Grand Prix order. The team generated huge excitement from the off with the sensational return of Michael Schumacher, but headlines soon followed on track: three podiums in their debut season, all via Nico Rosberg - who then claimed a breakthrough pole/victory double at China in 2012.


The following season he was paired with Lewis Hamilton, the duo going on to stage some epic title battles as the Silver Arrows swept all before them to become one of the most dominant forces of the modern F1 era. After Hamilton was partnered by steely Finn Valtteri Bottas, now the young and aspiring George Russel, who already had a chance to proof himself in Bahrain 2021, is now determined to show the 7-time World Champion what he is made of.


And even though Mercedes had a bumpy start into the 2022 season they still remain very much the team to beat…
